Spring Clean Up Details

Once the warm weather arrives, a spring cleanup keeps your landscape healthy and enhances the appearance of your property. Our yard cleanup service will help to bring your landscape back to life after enduring the harsh months of winter. Our services include picking up debris throughout your lawn, re-mulching your landscape bed, pulling weeds, and more. We can also cut back shrubs, perennials, and grasses, if needed. Our professional staff will take care of everything to prepare your yard for the warmer weather, as you begin to spend more time outdoors

Typically, a spring cleanup includes the following services:

  • Yard Cleanup - All debris from the winter will be cleaned up.
  • Weeding and Bed Maintenance - Weeds, limbs, and leftover leaves will be removed.
  • Landscape Edging - Your lawn and beds will be edged to give your property a defined, professional and well-maintained look.
  • Mulching - All bed areas will be mulched with an attractive and high-quality mulch.

Fall Clean Up Details

During the fall in Lake Tahoe, the trees shed their leaves and your yard becomes covered. Raking leaves is a tedious and time-consuming chore and can take time away from doing the wonderful fall activities that you love.

It may be tempting to let the fallen leaves lie where they are and hope that they decompose into mulch, but that usually causes more harm than good.

Leaving excess leaves on the lawn will block sunlight and reduce water evaporation. As a result, lawn suffocation, lack of photosynthesis, fungus, molds, and diseases can take root. Keeping your property free of dead and dying foliage with a fall cleanup, prevents insects and other pests from nesting in rotting debris in the winter, which can lead to issues when spring arrives.

We will not only remove all fallen leaves and debris thoroughly, but we’ll handle all the disposal for you as well. Fall is also the perfect time to get a jump start on planting and seeding for spring.

So, save yourself from the hard work of fall lawn care and let us do it for you!
